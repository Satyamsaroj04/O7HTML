HILL STATIONS
MANALI

Manali is a popular hill station in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, known for its stunning landscapes, adventure activities, and vibrant culture.

KASHMIR

Kashmir, often referred to as "Paradise on Earth," is renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, including snow-capped mountains, serene lakes, and lush valleys.

SPITI VALLEY

Spiti Valley is a cold desert mountain valley located in the Himalayas in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, known for its rugged terrain, ancient monasteries, and unique culture.
