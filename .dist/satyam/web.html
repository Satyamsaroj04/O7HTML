About Zero Hunger

Zero Hunger is a community-driven platform designed to connect local people with individuals and families who need help. Anyone can donate food, clothes, medicines, sanitary items or financial support. The platform uses WhatsApp notifications so users can instantly respond when someone nearby needs help.
